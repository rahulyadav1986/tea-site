INTRODUCTION

Fortunate to have been in constant contact with this magnificent culture for over 10 years, we can witness the special place tea has in the daily life of these inhabitants. This is how during our escapades, we had the pleasure of visiting many tea plantations and meeting their operators. Our passion for good tea growing, we decided to take the plunge, to create this website in order to share with you our experience and to help you find these wonderful products which, we are sure, will take you on a sensory journey conducive to development.

OUR MISSION

Created in 2020, le Comptoir's mission is to promote relaxation and meditation that are conducive to fulfilment by sharing, thanks to the fair trade of quality products and exclusive to the island of Taiwan, its tea culture and its ceremony.

To help you, we make the direct link between you and the producers that we have approached and who have decided to trust us, in order to offer you authentic products for sale.

OUR VALUES

Fair trade

It is by making the direct link between you and the producers that we can offer you the best products at an affordable price but also guarantee them a purchasing price that allows them to live and also to envisage the future.

Quality

It is in the fairness of our commercial relationship that we find one of the best ways to guarantee the quality of the products offered to you. Quality that is characterized by the agricultural practices and the care in the transformation, then, materializes during the tasting.

Transparency

On the one hand, we seek to offer you the best possible products, and on the other, we want to highlight the history and work of the people who give us the opportunity to immerse ourselves in the culture and tasting of these delicious teas.

Customer services

None of this is possible without services in line with our values. Our services are therefore proactive, aware of environmental issues and attentive to constructive criticism because we work to give you a pleasant experience.

OUR VISION

The whole of our company aims in the long term to promote the exchange between the people of their artisanal products, vectors of their respective cultures and identities, which can be transported over long distances without being compromised by the speed of transport. Indeed, we can observe that the artisanal products have an intrinsic value which will allow us, for example, to rely on a fleet of modern sailboats much more respectful of the environment for their transport.
